# Privacy Policy

**DaysUntil** — last updated 2026-04-29

## What we collect

**Nothing.** DaysUntil does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, makes no network requests, includes no analytics SDKs, and includes no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

## What's stored on your device

The events you create — title, date, emoji, and color — are stored locally in the app's sandboxed Documents directory. This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. DaysUntil receives only the entitlement status (purchased / not purchased). Apple's own privacy policy governs purchase information: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

## Children

DaysUntil is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not collect any data, so no data about children is ever processed.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear at this URL with the revised date.
