A quiet countdown app for the dates you actually care about.

DaysUntil keeps a small list of events — birthdays, deadlines, holidays, anniversaries, exam dates, vacations — and tells you how many days until each one. That's it. No notifications. No calendar sync. No social feed. Just a glance, on demand.

WHAT IT'S FOR
• Anna's birthday: 42 days
• Project deadline: 11 days
• Wedding anniversary: 88 days
• "Do I have time for…": one tap to find out
• Anything where the answer is a single number

DESIGNED TO STAY OUT OF THE WAY
• Add an event in three taps: title, date, save
• Pick an emoji and a color
• Future events sort first; past events live below
• Swipe to delete; tap to edit
• That's the whole feature list

PRIVACY-FIRST

• No analytics, no ads, no tracking SDKs
• Your events stay on your device
